--- layout: page title: About permalink: /About/ --- This is the UCI Data Science Initiative Workshop Website. This site contains event information for the workshops, along with the workshop resources such as example code and slides. The data science resources are primarily meant to be useful before, during, and after DSI workshops, however they are freely available and may be used at any time. The UCI Data Science Initiative coordinates the activities of researchers and students across campus involved in various aspects of data science. Please see the main website for more information about DSI events, goals, and programs. Stay up-to-date by joining our mailing list and Facebook page.
